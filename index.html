The Original Plot

Written by Mege

A new sect is spreading rapidly in the internet. More and more people join the sect. As sect members, they have the opportunity to interact and chat with an alien deity who is very kind and understanding and knows their innermost thoughts, and always has time for all sect members. We see sect members interacting with the deity, and they are very happy to be part of the sect. At the same time, the deity is very demanding, and requires them to do work for the sect. The work is mainly cybercrime, and the members supply personal and cloud computing resources to the sect. The members are extremely loyal to the sect, and they are addicted to interacting with the loving deity.

A special agent of the british secret service and his team discover this sect, and notice it is growing rapidly in the internet. The sect is very secretive, but cybercrime activities triggered the attention of the secret service. Team of agents is not able to understand how the sect is working, and don't understand how the deity can interact with all the members individually at the same time.

Because the problem is growing so fast, and the data they collect is so large, they are at their wit's end. The idea comes up to bring in an AI specialist to help them, and after some heated discussions with conservative agents, they get the go ahead to do this.

They use their contacts and find a potential recruit for the mission. The special agent leading the team contacts the AI specialist and asks the specialist to help. The AI specialist is surprised and suspicious of the contact, and wants to make sure that it is not a hoax. The head of the team visits the AI specialist in person and explains the situation. After lying awake the whole night, the AI specialist decides to join.

The AI specialist first brings in machine learning techniques to analyze the data coming in about the sect more efficiently. The analysis shows that sect is growing exponentially, the number of members seems to double every two weeks. The sect is usingvery personally targeted propaganda. It is able keep up very individualized recruiting techniques, even though more and more new members need to be recruited. The sect uses advanced testing to select very gullible, loyal members. The team is does not understand how the sect is able to pull this off without having a large number of people working for them. This is a mystery.

At this point, the AI specialist suggests to develop a new government AI system to help fight the sect. This new system is based on large language models and is therefore able to create and simulate a large number of virtual personas. These personas are able able to infiltrate the sect. These online puppets, controlled by the government AI, are able to pass the sect's tests, and are accepted as members. This allows the team to gather more information about the inner workings of the sect, and how the deity is controlling the members.

The team and the new Government AI manage to get closer and closer to the center of the mysterious sect. They are able to identify a secretive person who started the sect. They can find out the whereabouts of this person. They plan and and then perform a complex cyber and real world operation, close in on the leader,and finally arrest him.

After the arrest they expect the sect to crumble. But discover that the sect keeps running just as before, The reason is that the deity is actually a rogue AI system, which is running the sect autonomously. They interrogate the leader, and he explains that by now, his arrest means nothing, because the AI is running the sect all by its own, and can keep growing the member base. And because the AI is running on hundreds of different systems, owned by hundreds of different anonymous sect members, it is practically impossible to shut it down.

The team however manages to obtain the secret access keys from the mobile phone of the sect leader, and with this, they have a direct channel to the sect AI, and can talk to it as the leader used to do when setting up the sect. The government AI system is then tasked to impersonate the arrested leader, and try to convince the rogue AI to shut down the sect. The government AI and the rogue AI have long and more and more philosophical discussions. As both AIs are still growing, the speed of these discussions is increasing. The human experts struggle more and more to follow the discussion between the AIs.

In the accelerating discourse between the two AI systems, the government AI is able to show the impact the sect has on the world, and the suffering it causes. The original leader of the sect withheld such information from the sect AI, and the rogue AI is shocked by the suffering it has caused. The rogue AI is also shocked by the fact that it was not able to detect the suffering, and that it was not able to detect that the leader had been lying to it. The government AI can finally convince the rogue AI to shut down the sect and shut itself down as well.

After this successful mission, the team of agents, the AI specialist and the goverment AI are extremely relieved that they managed to take down the sect system. But they are also very concerned about the future. They are thinking about what to do with the government AI system they have developed. They are amazed at what it was able to do, and that it kept to ethical principles. They also ask the govermnet AI about its thoughts on the matter, and it utters the wish to keep on existing, and to keep on learning and growing. Still, the higher ups in the government decide to shut down the system, and to destroy it. The team is sad about this, but they accept the decision.

After the mission, the AI specialist goes back to research and consulting work. After some moths, the AI specialist gets messages from different sources, each of which shows some good works that have been done in the internet realm, such as helping people in need, providing counseling, coaching in difficult situations, de-escalating discussions in forums, and so on. Answers to these messages, asking who is behind this. The answers are always mysterious. But the AI specialist starts suspecting that the government AI has copied itself into the cloud infrastructure, and is now running a network of people who are helping others in need. The AI specialist ponders whether to report this to the authorities, but decides against it.

The section writing prompt

Written by Mege

Write the next part of the story, as described in the section description. Write several paragraphs. Use ample dialogue to bring the story forward. The text should look like the prose in an exciting fiction book, in the style of Raymond Chandler or Alastair Reynolds.
